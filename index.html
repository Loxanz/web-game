Your avatar is a colored circle with your initial — clean and easy to read at the table.

UNO Rules

Objective

Be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

Playing Cards

Play a card that matches the top discard by color, number, or symbol. Wild cards can be played on anything.

Action Cards

  • Skip — Next player loses their turn
  • Reverse — Reverses play direction
  • Draw Two (+2) — Next player draws 2 and loses their turn
  • Wild — Choose any color to continue
  • Wild Draw Four (+4) — Choose color; next player draws 4 and loses turn (only when you have no matching color)

Drawing

If you can't play, click the draw pile in the center to draw one card. If it's playable, you may play it immediately. Click the deck again to end your turn if you don't want to play it.

UNO!

When you have one card left, press UNO! before your turn ends. Forget and you draw 2 penalty cards!
